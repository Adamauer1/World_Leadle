    How to Play!
    The goal of the game is to find the name of the person that is displayed on the left. You have 5 guesses to come up with this persons name. Each guess will display helpful information that will help point you in the right direction of the answer. There are five catigories that will be displayed, name, title, country, continent, and finally century. Name displays the person name, Title shows their highest title held, Country shows the country that they held the position in, Continent shows the main continent that the country is in, century shows the centuries that the person held that position.
